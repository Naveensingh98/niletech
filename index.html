ABOUT US

Welcome to Niletech Infrabuild Pvt. Ltd.

Niletech Infrabuild Pvt. Ltd.is a prominent environmental solutions provider delivering advanced technologies for effective and sustainable wastewater management. Our organisation is driven by a skilled and experienced team committed to enhancing environmental quality across domestic and industrial sectors.
We specialise in in-situ bioremediation, employing scientifically developed microbial formulations that accelerate pollutant degradation and restore water quality with minimal infrastructure requirements. This nature-aligned approach offers a reliable, energy-efficient and economically advantageous alternative to conventional treatment systems.
By integrating innovative microbial processes with sound environmental engineering principles, our solutions assist industries and municipal bodies in achieving regulatory compliance while lowering operational costs and reducing ecological footprints. Through this commitment, Niletech Infrabuild Pvt. Ltd. continues to advance high-performance, sustainable and future-oriented wastewater treatment technologies.
